How to game more?

126 words | 1 minute read

Six hours a day you are at school listening to teachers, making your homework and chatting with your friends. When you come home you eat something and make some more homework. When you are finished you sometimes have some time left to game. But that is not always the case. That makes us sad, because we think that gaming with your friends is lovely.

Our strategy for more gaming in a week is to make more homework at school. I know that that sound weird, I mean the name is homework, not school work. But if you are foccused at your lessons you can make all your homework very quick. This means there is more time to game and that makes us happy!
