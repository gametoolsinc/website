Introduction

Hanbury Brown Twiss Experiment

Hanbury Brown and Twiss (HBT) effect is any of a variety of correlation and anti-correlation effects in the intensities received by two detectors from a beam of particles.

Question

What does the HBT experiment prove?

The experiment checks whether there is a correlation or anti-correlation effect between the intensities received by two detectors from a beam of particles.
