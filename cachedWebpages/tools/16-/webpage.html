Introduction

Yu Gi Oh Scoreboard

Yu Gi Oh Scoreboard is a scoreboard for keeping track of the score of you and your opponent during a match of Yu Gi Oh. This scoreboard also features some helpful tools, like a dice and a coin flipper for random results, but a button to show the history of the entire match. The latter of which you can use to check what has happened over the course of the Yu Gi Oh match. Don’t worry, we’ve got an undo button in case you made a mistake.

Question

Why can't you just track your scores on a piece of paper?

This is definitely an option, but is not the best option available. Our tool is all very organised, so minimal mistakes will be made by calculating your scores. As a bonus feature you also have access to a random dice thrower and coin flipper.

Example

From my start score 5389 is subtracted, how much will I have left?

Your start score is always 8000. Your score gets subtracted by 5389, so your new score will be 8000 - 5389 = 2611.
